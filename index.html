Our Story

Full-Service Site Development, Done Right

Warrior Environmental and Mining Services is a full-service site development contractor specializing in coal mine job site preparation. Our expertise includes overburden removal, haul road construction, sediment and water control structures, site grading, and environmental reclamation. We partner with mining operations to deliver precise, efficient, and environmentally responsible solutions that support both production goals and regulatory compliance.

Our team is equipped with advanced machinery and technology to handle projects of all sizes — from initial land clearing and precise satellite-guided grading to pond management and site restoration. Whether you're preparing a site for new construction or reclaiming land for environmental compliance, we deliver reliable, high-quality results that meet strict regulatory standards and project timelines.

Meet the Team

A Family Business, Rooted in Brookwood

Tim Hagood was born and raised in Brookwood, AL. As the son of a local minister, the values of family, faith, and community were instilled at an early age. Tim values the Brookwood community and understands the long, rich history of the coal mining industry in the area.

As the owner of Warrior Environmental and Mining Services, Tim provides jobs to the community and partners with the mining industry to deliver safe and environmentally responsible solutions to mining and related projects.

Tim's son Kyle joined the business several years ago, and today he manages the day-to-day operations. He, too, is dedicated to sustainable practices in order to meet the needs of mining and related projects in a safe and effective manner. Warrior Environmental and Mining Services strives to surround itself with employees who are team players in fulfilling these goals.
